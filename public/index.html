❄️ ice-vault


>A crypto wallet is secure by design, only you can spend your wallet.
>But what happens when the unexpected happens, like when you cease to exist.
>No one can access your wallet, not even your closest ones…


Sharing wallet seed phrase is maybe the first thing that comes to most people’s mind, but it involves a huge risk. Anyone who knows your seed phrase will immediately have a full control of your wallet! This contract is designed so that you can share your hard earned assets to persons, selected by you, only after you passed without compromising security.

This contract generates a unique vault for each user where you can freeze your funds and then it will melt when the time comes, which is after a deadline. As long as you interact with the vault, the deadline will be postponed. A person who holds a key token will then be able to redeem the funds after the vault has melted.

With this contract you can:
Create Vault, Create Pocket, Update Vault, Update Pocket, Delete Pocket,
Mint Vault Key, Mint Pocket Key, Burn Vault Key, Burn Pocket Key,
Deposit Funds, Withdraw Funds, Redeem Funds

Create Vault

Mints VaultLock and VaultKey, a new address will be generated. Send VaultLock to that newly generated address. Keep VaultKey in your wallet, and/or send them to your other wallets. You will need to show the token when doing other actions later.

Redeem assets from a certain pocket when the deadline has passed by showing the corresponding PocketKey token.
